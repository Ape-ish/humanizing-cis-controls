# Risk Management Framework (RMF)

RMF is a 6-step process developed by NIST to manage information system risk.

## 6 Steps of RMF
1. **Categorize**
2. **Select**
3. **Implement**
4. **Assess**
5. **Authorize**
6. **Monitor**
